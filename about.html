About Gallaudet

Gallaudet University

Gallaudet University is the world leader in liberal education and career development for deaf and hard of hearing students. The University enjoys an international reputation for its outstanding programs and for the quality of the research it conducts on the history, language, culture, and other topics related to deaf people.

About the Honors Program

The Honors Program at Gallaudet University

The Honors Program is a two-tier program offering students advanced preparation for graduate school or professional employment. We offer General Studies Honors (where students work towards Honors completing their General Studies Requirements) and Capstone Honors (where students work towards an Honors degree during their last two years and complete an Honors Capstone). Students who graduate with Capstone Honors will be eminently prepared for admission to top-tier graduate or professional programs.
To aid students in their quest for excellence, we provide a number of benefits including dedicated study facilities, scholarships, social programs, and enhanced academic preparation.
If you have questions about our program, please contact honors@gallaudet.edu.
